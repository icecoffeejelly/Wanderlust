Mia Bella! It’s Firenze Gelateria




Whether it’s ice cream, gelato or frozen yogurt that strikes your fancy, Jakarta’s weather always warrants a good time to have an icy treat. Located in Gading Serpong, Firenze Gelateria serves up gelato inspired by all things Italian in a playful setting.



Firenze Gelateria offers classic Italian flavours such as Amarena cherry, Straciatella (chunky chocolate chip) and Cioccolato. Of course, if you’re in the mood for something different, there are also cheese and seasonal flavour such as pumpkin to choose from.

The two-floor gelateria is decked out in pink theme interior with gold and sandy coloured accent. Over at the second floor, a round window overlooking the main road is the most popular spot in the establishment. The feminine interior is obviously targeted towards females but Firenze Gelateria’s laidback ambiance often attracts young teenagers who spend their time discussing homework with the company of gelato.



Early morning and late afternoon are the periods when Firenze Gelateria is often packed with young kids from nearby school getting their much-needed treats from parents. And if that were an atmosphere you wouldn’t mind to be a part in, then the lively crowd would suit you nicely.
